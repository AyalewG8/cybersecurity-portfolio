# Northbridge Logistics — Cybersecurity Risk Assessment

## Project Summary

This project evaluates cybersecurity risk for a logistics company with approximately 150 employees across three offices and a hybrid environment using on-premises and cloud systems.

## Business Problem

The organization observed phishing attempts, unauthorized login activity, and malware indicators. The assessment identifies critical assets, threats, vulnerabilities, business impacts, and practical controls.

## Objectives

- Identify and prioritize information assets
- Evaluate likely threats and vulnerabilities
- Estimate likelihood and business impact
- Recommend administrative, technical, and physical controls
- Design a defense-in-depth security architecture
- Improve incident response and operational resilience

## Recommended Controls

- Identity and access management with RBAC
- Multifactor authentication for privileged and remote access
- SIEM monitoring and SOAR-supported response workflows
- Endpoint detection and response
- Secure email gateway with SPF, DKIM, and DMARC
- Network segmentation and least-privilege access
- Tested backups, business continuity, and disaster recovery plans
- Security awareness and phishing simulation training

## Deliverables

- Executive summary
- Asset inventory and risk register
- Threat and vulnerability analysis
- Risk treatment plan
- Security architecture diagram
- Incident response recommendations
- Final presentation
