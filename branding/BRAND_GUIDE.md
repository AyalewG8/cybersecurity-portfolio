# Ayalew Cherenet — Cybersecurity Portfolio Brand Guide

## Brand Position
A service-driven cybersecurity and information technology professional focused on risk management, cloud security, secure architecture, vulnerability management, and SOC operations.

## Core Message
**Protecting systems. Solving problems. Helping people.**

## Color Palette
- Midnight Navy: `#07111F`
- Executive Navy: `#0B1F3A`
- Gold Accent: `#C9A227`
- Cyber Teal: `#49D6C7`
- Slate: `#A7B8CA`
- White: `#FFFFFF`

## Typography
- Headings: Space Grotesk, Poppins, or Aptos Display
- Body: Inter, Aptos, or Calibri
- Code/Technical Labels: JetBrains Mono or Consolas

## Visual Language
Use clean grids, subtle network lines, shield and cloud motifs, generous spacing, restrained gradients, and high-contrast executive layouts. Avoid clutter, excessive stock imagery, and decorative effects that reduce readability.

## Professional Headline
**Cybersecurity Analyst | Information Technology | Risk Management | Cloud Security | SOC Operations**
